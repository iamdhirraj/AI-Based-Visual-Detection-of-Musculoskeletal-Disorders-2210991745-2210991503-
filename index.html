Notebook flow

What the notebook does

1

Load and label the dataset

Walks the MURA directory structure, reads CSV path lists, and maps positive/negative labels.

2

Preprocess images

Resizes radiographs to 256 px, normalizes pixel intensity, and optionally applies simple augmentation.

3

Train multiple architectures

Benchmarks custom CNNs, VGG16/VGG19, ResNet152V2/ResNet50, and several DenseNet201 variants.

4

Evaluate and compare

Tracks AUC, accuracy, and Cohen’s kappa, then saves the trained models for later ensembling.
